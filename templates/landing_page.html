How it works

From packet to blocked — in milliseconds

Four stages run in parallel, continuously, on every connection.

1
Capture traffic
Every TCP/UDP/ICMP packet is captured and assembled into connection flows.
2
Extract features
41 statistical features computed per flow: bytes, duration, flags, protocol type.
3
ML classifies
Random Forest labels each flow: Normal, DoS, Port Scan, Brute Force and more.
4
Auto-respond
Attacks blocked via iptables. Admins get email alerts. Everything logged.

DoS / DDoS

Malicious SQL in form inputs to extract or destroy your database.
